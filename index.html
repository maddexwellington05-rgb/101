Wax Museum Records

The coast's crate-digging stop

Inside Vintage 101 you will find Wax Museum Records and MCM Vintage: alphabetized flip bins of vinyl, framed album art, music posters at three for fifty, and a wall of original show flyers. From Pet Sounds to Kraftwerk, new arrivals land all the time.
