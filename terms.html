About our inventory

Vintage 101 is a marketplace of antiques and one of a kind finds. More than twenty independent vendors keep their own booths inside the shop, and the furniture department, Furniture 101, sits right next door. Because almost everything we carry is vintage and one of a kind, our stock is always changing.

  • An item you see on this website may already be sold, set aside, or no longer in the shop.
  • Availability, condition, and prices can change at any time without notice.
  • Vendors restock, trade, and rotate their booths often, so the shop looks a little different on every visit.

If you are hoping to find a specific piece, the surest way to check is to call us at (541) 808-0980 or stop in. We are open daily, 11:00 am to 5:00 pm.
