Wander the booths

Every booth its own little world

More than twenty independent vendors fill the marketplace, each keeping their own corner. No two booths look alike, and nothing stays the same for long. Vendors restock, trade, and rotate, so the shop looks a little different every time you come back. Take it slow and let a shelf or a case catch your eye.
