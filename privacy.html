Privacy Policy

Vintage 101 Marketplace is a small, family run shop in Coos Bay, Oregon. This website is a simple informational page about the store. There is no online store, no visitor accounts, and no newsletter signup here. This policy explains, in plain terms, what little information the site touches and how we handle it.

Last updated: June 2026

We keep this short and honest because the site is simple. You can browse every page without giving us any information, without creating an account, and without signing up for anything. We do not run advertising trackers, and we do not sell or share your personal information. If anything about our practices changes, we will update this page.

Information the site collects

Browsing this website does not require you to share any personal information. We do not ask you to register, log in, or fill out forms to read about the shop, the vendors, or our hours.

If you choose to email or call us using the contact details on this site, you are sharing your own contact information with us directly. We use that information only to reply to you and to help with whatever you reached out about. We do not add you to a mailing list, and we do not pass your message along to anyone outside the shop.

How we use what you send us

When you contact us by email or phone, here is what that looks like:

  • We read your message or take your call and respond to your question.
  • We keep your email or note only as long as it is useful for answering you and following up.
  • We do not use your contact information for advertising, and we do not trade or sell it.

Selling and sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share it with advertisers or data brokers. The only people who see an email or message you send are the family and staff who run Vintage 101.

Advertising and trackers

This site does not use third party advertising trackers, and it is not built to follow you around the web. It is an informational page about a brick and mortar shop, nothing more. We do not invent or attach analytics services or marketing pixels that are not actually here.

The staff login

The only login on this website is a private staff login. It exists so the shop owner can sign in to update site content, such as hours, photos, and vendor listings. This login is not for visitors. There are no public accounts to create, and nothing on the public pages asks you to register or sign in.

Embedded content from other companies

Some parts of this site connect to services run by other companies. When you use those features, the other company's own privacy practices apply, not ours. For example:

  • Maps and directions on this site are provided through Google Maps, which is governed by Google's privacy policy.
  • Links to our Facebook and Instagram pages take you to those platforms, which are governed by their own privacy policies.

We share these so you can find us and follow along. We do not control how those companies handle information once you are on their pages, so we encourage you to review their policies.

Children's privacy

This website is meant for a general audience and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If a child has sent us their contact information by email and a parent or guardian would like it removed, please reach out using the details below and we will take care of it.

How to contact us about privacy

If you have any questions about this policy or about how we handle a message you sent us, please get in touch. We are a small shop and happy to help.
